UI design

xixixi

Once I tested out all usability mistakes, I started designing the final screens in Sketch.

  • What kind of visual style did you follow? (Fresh, corporate, dark, light?)
  • Did you follow any guidelines? (Material Design, iOS Styleguide, etc?)
  • What platforms did you design for?
  • Which details really fill you with pride?
  • What inspired you to use this style?

Interviews

xixixi

At the discovery phase of my project, I conducted user interviews in order to get a better understanding of the problem.

  • What were the research questions? What were you curious about the most?
  • How many users did you interview?
  • What were the main insights you got from the interviews?
  • What did you change on the design based on the insights?

Design System

xixixi

As we implemented new features into the product, the project got more and more complex. We decided to set up a Design System in order to have a unified set of rules that we can follow.

  • Why did you decide to work with a design system?
  • What did you include in the design system? UI elements, animations, interactions?
  • What was the most challenging part of designing this system?
  • Did you use any frameworks to create the Design System (Atomic Design principles, etc)?

Sketches

xixixi

I usually start the design process with low fidelity wireframes. This is the way I iterate through many design options quickly.

  • What was the main purpose of your sketches? (presenting, testing, brainstorming?)
  • How many different versions were made?
  • Which version did you choose to go on with and why?

Card sorting

xixixi

I decided on the main categories of the webshop with a Card Sorting session

  • What was your main purpose? What navigation problems did the website have?
  • What type of card sorting method did you work with? (Closed or reversed, online or in person)
  • How did the participants gather the information?
  • What kind of new navigation structure did you suggest?

  • At what point did you decide that you need personas?
  • What information did you provide about each of the personas? (demographics, problems, motivations, etc)

Competetive research

xixixi

I gathered information about the competition in order to forecast a potential for the market.

  • How did the Competitive Research affect the overall strategy?
  • What aspects have you considered while doing the Competitive Research?
  • What products were the main competitors (direct and indirect)?
  • What were the advantages and disadvantages of competing products? Did you find a niche market?

Heuristic review

xixixi

As the first step of the redesign process, I evaluated the website with the heuristic review method.

  • On what criteria did you evaluate the design?
  • What were the biggest problems that you found?
  • What kind of suggestions did you make based on these problems?

Customer Journey

xixixi

In order to get to know our users' behaviour better, we created a Customer Journey Map.

  • What was the whole buying process from the point when the user do not even know the product? What happens after using the product?
  • What were the main touchpoints where the user contacted with the service?
  • What were the main painpoints for the user during the use of the service?
  • What functions, solutions did you find out to treat these painpoints?

User journey/flow

xixixi

I devised three version for the user journey of the same task and tested them with prototypes to find out which one works the best.

  • How did you determine the process?
  • What did the user feel about each step of the process?
  • How did you test the process?

Key performance indicators

xixixi

We agreed that the most important task was to increase the retention rate since it was the product's most important KPI.

  • What quantifiable goals did you want to achieve with your design?
  • How did you measure these goals?
  • What method did you use to define these goals?
  • What have you changed in the design to this end?

Business requirements and goals

xixixi

The company wanted to increase new user sales conversion by 15% without affecting retention.

  • What challenges did your client face?
  • What were the main trends in the field?

Look & Feel

xixixi

As a first part of the UI design process, I presented three different Look & Feel plans to the client which helped us narrowing down the options to the one that they really liked.

  • How many different Look & Feel versions did you make?
  • What was the main difference between the versions?
  • What was the reason for choosing the one that they liked?

User Testing

xixixi

Before launching the product, I did a testing round in order to reveal possible usability problems.

  • At which phase of the project did you test and why?
  • Who were the test subjects? Did they meet the target group?
  • What method did you use for testing? (Remote, moderated, etc?)
  • What tasks did you give to the test subjects?
  • What were the most important remarks? What changed in the design?
